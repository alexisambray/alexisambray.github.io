Laboratory Lockdown Game

A 3D Unity game that helps Grade 6 students in the Philippines master both lower- and higher-order learning outcomes on mixtures through interactive gameplay and real-world simulations. The lower-order learning outcome is for students to describe the use and appearance of homogeneous and heterogeneous mixtures. The higher-order learning outcome is for students to demonstrate the separation techniques such as winnowing, evaporation, magnetic, decantation, filtration, sieving, picking, and distillation. This game is developed, designed, and planned with a story plot by my thesis mates Elizabeth Celestino, Michelle Martinez, Miguel Singson, and me.

  • Tools: Unity, C#, Adobe Illustrator, Canva
